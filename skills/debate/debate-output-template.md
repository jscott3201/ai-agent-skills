# Debate Output Template

```markdown
# [Topic] Decision Debate

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Question:** [The specific decision being evaluated]
**Method:** [N]-perspective debate, [N] rounds
**Status:** Complete

## The Decision

[1-2 paragraphs framing the decision: what needs to be decided, what
constraints exist, what the stakes are.]

## Perspectives

| # | Perspective | Frame | Optimizes For |
|---|-------------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | [Role] | [Reasoning frame] | [What this perspective prioritizes] |
| 2 | [Role] | [Reasoning frame] | [What this perspective prioritizes] |
| 3 | [Role] | [Reasoning frame] | [What this perspective prioritizes] |
| 4 | Devil's Advocate | [Alternative frame] | Surface hidden risks and alternatives |

## Phase 1: Independent Positions

### [Perspective 1]: [Role Name]

**Claim:** [Position statement]
**Grounds:** [Evidence - benchmarks, data, experience]
**Warrant:** [Why the evidence supports the claim]
**Qualifier:** [Degree of certainty and conditions]
**Rebuttal:** [When this position is wrong]

### [Perspective 2]: [Role Name]

[Same structure]

## Phase 2: Exchange

### Round 1 (Adversarial)

**[Perspective 1] challenges [Perspective 2]:**
[Strongest counterargument and response]

**[Perspective 2] challenges [Perspective 1]:**
[Strongest counterargument and response]

### Round 2 (Revision)

**[Perspective 1] revised position:**
[What changed and why, or "Position unchanged because..."]

**[Perspective 2] revised position:**
[What changed and why]

## Phase 3: Synthesis

### Scoring

| Perspective | Evidence | Relevance | Rebuttal | Reversibility | Calibration | Total |
|-------------|----------|-----------|----------|---------------|-------------|-------|
| [Role 1] | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /25 |
| [Role 2] | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /25 |
| [Role 3] | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /25 |
| Devil's Advocate | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /5 | /25 |

### Consensus Items

[Findings where 3+ perspectives agree - highest confidence]

1. [Consensus finding]
2. [Consensus finding]

### Key Debates

#### Debate 1: [Point of disagreement]

**[Side A] argues:** [Position with strongest evidence]
**[Side B] argues:** [Counter-position with strongest evidence]
**Ruling:** [Decision and the specific evidence that tipped the balance]

### Bias Check

- Anchoring: [Was the first perspective disproportionately influential? Y/N]
- Sunk cost: [Did any perspective argue from prior investment? Y/N]
- Groupthink: [Did perspectives converge without genuine tension? Y/N]
- Optimism: [Were effort/risk estimates realistic? Y/N]

[Note any bias detected and how it was accounted for]

## Decision

**Recommendation:** [Clear recommendation]
**Confidence:** [High/Medium/Low]
**Conditions:** [What must be true for this recommendation to hold]
**Revisit if:** [Conditions that would invalidate this decision]

## Alternatives Considered

[Each losing position preserved with its strongest argument, so future
decision-makers understand why it was rejected without re-debating]

- **[Alternative A]:** [Why rejected - specific weakness or tradeoff]
- **[Alternative B]:** [Why rejected]

## Residual Disagreements

[What remains unresolved. These are not failures - they are documented
uncertainties that may need future investigation.]

| Disagreement | Perspectives | Resolution Path |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| [Unresolved point] | [Who disagrees] | [How/when this gets resolved] |

## Deferred Items

| Item | Gate | Source |
|------|------|--------|
| [Deferred work surfaced by debate] | [What must be true first] | [Which perspective raised it] |
```
